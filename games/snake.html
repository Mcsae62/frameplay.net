Snake

Game Description

Snake is a classic arcade game where players control a growing snake on a map. The objective is to eat food to make the snake longer while avoiding collisions with walls and the snake's own body. While easy to learn, achieving a high score requires skill and strategy.

  • Progressive difficulty increase

Tips & Strategies

  • Stay calm to avoid directional mistakes
  • Move along the walls for better path planning
  • Use zigzag patterns in open spaces to maximize scoring
  • Anticipate food placement and plan your route ahead
  • Be extra careful not to trap yourself when the snake gets longer
