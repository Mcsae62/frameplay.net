Sushi Roll

Game Description

You are shrimply the best! In this endless-runner you whiz straight through the asian kitchen with one of many super tasty sushi-rolls. But be careful! They are not there to be eaten. Try to collect as many coins as you can, unlock new sushi-skins, compete with other players from all over the world and sign up for the leaderboard! Lovely designed levels and tricky sections will await you. Show your skill and try to be the best! How far will you get?

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or touch controls to roll your sushi
  • Collect coins for points and rewards
  • Avoid obstacles in the kitchen
  • Unlock new sushi roll skins
  • Navigate through tricky sections
  • Compete on the global leaderboard
  • Try to roll as far as possible

Game Features

  • Multiple sushi roll skins to unlock
  • Global leaderboard system
  • Beautiful Asian kitchen setting
  • Challenging obstacle courses
  • Coin collection system
  • Endless runner gameplay
  • Suitable for all ages

Tips & Strategies

  • Learn the timing of obstacles
  • Collect coins whenever possible
  • Practice smooth rolling control
  • Watch out for kitchen hazards
  • Use power-ups strategically
  • Study the best routes
  • Start slow and gradually increase speed
