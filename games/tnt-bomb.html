TNT Bomb

Game Description

Explosions, explosions and guess what - even more explosions! Dive into 'TNT Bomb' and let your vandalism run wild once again. You have to destroy several buildings and structures with 5 mighty power-ups. Use a cleverly devised strategy and be flexible because everything is limited. But if nothing else helps, then use the ultimate earthquake. That should do the rest! Get overwhelmed by beautiful graphics and well-designed levels as you become the Master of destruction!

How to Play

  • Click or tap to place TNT bombs strategically
  • Use different power-ups to maximize destruction
  • Destroy the required percentage of structures
  • Chain explosions for bigger destruction
  • Complete levels to unlock new challenges
  • Use the earthquake power-up when needed

Game Features

  • Immersive 3D Arcade gameplay
  • Stunning graphics and visual effects
  • 5 powerful power-ups to unlock
  • Multiple challenging levels
  • Physics-based destruction system
  • Suitable for all ages
  • Progressive difficulty

Tips & Strategies

  • Study the structure before placing bombs
  • Target weak points for maximum damage
  • Save power-ups for challenging situations
  • Use chain reactions to your advantage
  • Plan your moves to meet level objectives
  • Save the earthquake power-up for tough spots
  • Look for opportunities to create combo explosions
