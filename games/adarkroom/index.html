A Dark Room

How to Play A Dark Room

A Dark Room is a minimalist text-based adventure game developed by Michael Townsend. Here's how to play:

  • Start by lighting the fire and gathering wood to keep it burning
  • As you progress, you'll unlock new buildings, gather resources, and manage a growing village
  • Eventually, you'll be able to explore the world outside your room
  • The game gradually reveals its story as you discover new areas and items
  • Pay attention to the subtle narrative clues throughout the game

Controls:

  • Mouse: Click on buttons and options to perform actions
  • During exploration: Use arrow keys or on-screen buttons to move

Tips:

  • Balance resource collection with exploration
  • Be patient - the game reveals itself slowly
  • Make sure to check all available options as new ones appear
  • The game autosaves your progress, so you can return later
