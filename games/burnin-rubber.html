Burnin Rubber

Game Description

Choose your car, choose your weapon and head out to rule the streets. Steer by tapping left or right and burn some rubber. Experience the thrill of street racing with customizable vehicles and powerful weapons. Master the art of drifting and combat as you compete against rival racers in this action-packed racing game.

How to Play

Tap left or right to steer your car

Use weapons to take down opponents

Collect power-ups and upgrades

Master drifting techniques

Avoid obstacles and rival attacks

Complete missions to earn rewards

Game Features

Multiple customizable cars

Various weapons and power-ups

Exciting street racing action

Drifting mechanics

Mission-based gameplay

Suitable for all ages

Tips & Strategies

Practice smooth steering for better control

Use weapons strategically against opponents

Learn to drift around corners efficiently

Collect power-ups whenever possible

Upgrade your car's performance regularly

Study track layouts for optimal racing lines
