Pacman

Game Description

Pacman is one of the most iconic arcade games of the 1980s. Created by Namco in 1980, it quickly became one of the most popular video games worldwide. Players control a yellow circular character moving through a maze, eating dots while avoiding four different colored ghosts. The simplicity and addictive gameplay made it a classic in video game history.

How to Play

Pacman rules are simple and straightforward:

  • Use arrow keys to control Pacman through the maze
  • Eat all the dots in the maze while avoiding ghosts
  • Eat power pellets (larger flashing dots) to temporarily eat ghosts
  • Complete the level by eating all dots

Controls:

  • Arrow keys: Move Pacman up, down, left, right
  • P key: Pause the game

Game Features

  • Classic original Pacman gaming experience
  • Authentic graphics and sound effects
  • Simple to learn but difficult to master gameplay
  • Four colorful ghosts with different behavior patterns
  • Increasing difficulty as levels progress
  • Occasional fruit bonuses

Tips & Strategies

  • Learn each ghost's behavior: Red chases, Pink intercepts, Cyan ambushes, Orange moves randomly
  • Use the maze pathways and corners to evade ghosts
  • Lure ghosts close before eating power pellets to maximize ghost-eating opportunities
  • In advanced levels, power pellets have shorter durations, use them wisely
  • Pay attention to ghost release timing from the center area to plan your route
  • Remember the teleport tunnels on both sides of the maze for quick escapes
