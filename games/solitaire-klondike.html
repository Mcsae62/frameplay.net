Solitaire Klondike

Game Description

Welcome to Solitaire Klondike, a timeless patience card game that has captivated millions of players worldwide! This classic single-player game challenges both your strategic thinking and patience as you work to build four foundation piles from Ace to King. With its intuitive controls, multiple game variations including Vegas mode, and beautiful customizable card designs, this version of Klondike Solitaire offers the perfect blend of traditional gameplay and modern features. Whether you're a seasoned player or new to the game, you'll find hours of entertainment as you strategically move cards and unlock winning combinations!

How to Play

  • Start with 7 tableau piles and 4 empty foundation piles
  • Build descending sequences with alternating colors on tableau
  • Create ascending foundation piles from Ace to King by suit
  • Use the stock pile to draw additional cards
  • Move cards between piles strategically
  • Tap cards to automatically send them to foundation piles
  • Win by completing all four foundation piles

Game Features

  • Classic Klondike and Vegas Solitaire modes
  • Multiple beautiful card designs
  • Customizable table backgrounds
  • Helpful undo function
  • Smart hint system
  • Auto-complete feature
  • Completely free to play

Tips & Strategies

  • Always try to reveal face-down cards first
  • Keep foundation piles balanced as you build them
  • Create empty columns for temporary card storage
  • Plan several moves ahead
  • Use the undo function to explore different strategies
  • Don't rush to move cards to foundation piles
  • Try Vegas mode for an extra challenge
