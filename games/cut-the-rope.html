Cut The Rope

Feed the cute monster Om Nom with candy by cutting ropes and solving challenging physics puzzles. Collect stars and discover the perfect timing in this addictive puzzle game!

How to Play

  • Swipe or click to cut the ropes
  • Guide the candy into Om Nom's mouth
  • Collect as many stars as possible in each level
  • Use physics elements like air cushions, bubbles, and spikes to your advantage
  • Complete levels to unlock new boxes with more challenges
  • Try to collect all three stars in each level for a perfect score

Game Features

  • Engaging physics-based gameplay
  • Cute character and colorful graphics
  • Multiple boxes with unique level themes
  • Progressive difficulty and new gameplay elements
  • Various obstacles and puzzle mechanisms
  • Challenging star collection system

Tips & Strategies

  • Observe the level before cutting any ropes
  • Plan your sequence of cuts carefully
  • Timing is crucial for many puzzles
  • Use momentum to swing the candy where you need it
  • Some stars require very specific rope cutting sequences
  • Don't be afraid to retry levels to perfect your strategy

About the Game

  • Perfect for all ages with its intuitive controls and charming design
  • Runs smoothly on all devices
  • No download required - play instantly in your browser
