Truck Trials

Game Description

This racing game will not only challenge your courage to go fast, but at the same time demand that you try to be as skillful as possible while doing so. There are lots of obstacles that you have to crush, drive through, jump over or slip under. This is probably the best racing game if you have some serious anger management issues. Just crush a few cars, explode some highly volatile barrels and you will feel relieved and happy again. Simple as that.

You should limit outbreaks like this to this truck racing game, however. Doing this in real life with your hatchback might not be such a good idea. And while you're crushing and jumping and speeding through the various levels, don't forget to collect as many bonus bags as possible. These will give you extra points that ultimately will add up to your overall score.

The goal of the game is to finish each of the 20 unique trial racing levels with the maximum amount of points and of course with three stars. If you're not able to reach three stars in the earlier levels, be aware that each new truck you unlock will be a little faster than the last. So why not try already beaten levels with a new truck again and gain more points?

This truck racer will blow you away with fantastic levels, brute force and an iconic Heavy Metal soundtrack to get you in the right mood for crushing stuff!

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to control your truck
  • Crush obstacles and cars
  • Jump over barriers
  • Collect bonus bags for extra points
  • Complete levels with three stars
  • Unlock new, faster trucks
  • Try to achieve high scores

Game Features

  • 20 challenging levels
  • High scores system
  • New trucks to unlock
  • Bonus points collection
  • Heavy Metal soundtrack
  • Multiple obstacle types
  • Three-star rating system

Tips & Strategies

  • Master truck control and physics
  • Learn optimal paths through obstacles
  • Collect all bonus bags for maximum points
  • Use unlocked trucks to improve scores
  • Practice difficult sections
  • Balance speed and control
  • Study level layouts for best routes
