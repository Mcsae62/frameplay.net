8 Ball Billiards Classic

Experience realistic physics and smooth gameplay in this classic pool game. Play against the computer or challenge your friends!

How to Play

  • Aim using your mouse or touch
  • Adjust power by dragging back further
  • Try to pocket all of your assigned balls (solid or striped)
  • Pocket the 8-ball last to win the game
  • Be careful not to pot the 8-ball early or you lose
  • Avoid scratching (pocketing the cue ball)

Game Features

  • Realistic physics simulation
  • Smooth and precise ball control
  • Single player against AI opponent
  • Beautiful table graphics and effects
  • Official 8-ball pool rules
  • Intuitive aiming and power system

Tips & Strategies

  • Plan your shots ahead - think about positioning for your next shot
  • Use gentle power for better control
  • Try to leave the cue ball in a good position after each shot
  • Sometimes it's better to play defensively than to attempt a difficult shot
  • Learn to use spin (english) to control where the cue ball goes
  • Pay attention to blocking your opponent's shots

About the Game

  • 8 Ball Billiards Classic is a realistic pool simulation game
  • Based on the official rules of 8-ball pool
  • Perfect for both casual players and billiards enthusiasts
  • Runs smoothly on all devices
  • No download required - play instantly in your browser
  • Test your skills against challenging AI opponents
