Jigsaw Puzzle Deluxe

Game Description

Welcome to Jigsaw Puzzle Deluxe, an enhanced version of the classic puzzle game featuring stunning motives and multiple challenge levels! This deluxe edition offers six different difficulty settings, ranging from relaxing casual sessions to intense hardcore rounds. Each puzzle presents an opportunity to earn up to 3 stars, testing your puzzle-solving skills across various complexity levels. Whether you're looking for a casual gaming experience or seeking to prove yourself as a puzzle master, this game delivers the perfect balance of challenge and entertainment.

How to Play

  • Select your preferred difficulty level
  • Choose from various beautiful motives
  • Drag and drop pieces to their correct positions
  • Complete puzzles with 81, 144, or 256 pieces
  • Try rotate mode for extra challenge
  • Collect stars for perfect completions
  • Progress through increasingly difficult levels

Game Features

  • Beautiful puzzle motives
  • Six difficulty levels
  • Three piece counts: 81, 144, and 256
  • Challenging rotate mode
  • Star collection system
  • Smooth drag-and-drop controls
  • Progressive difficulty system

Tips & Strategies

  • Start with edge pieces to create a frame
  • Group pieces by color and pattern
  • Begin with easier difficulties to learn mechanics
  • Use the preview image as reference
  • In rotate mode, focus on piece orientation first
  • Take breaks during challenging puzzles
  • Practice to improve completion times
