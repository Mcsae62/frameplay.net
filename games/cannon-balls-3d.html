Cannon Balls 3D

Game Description

Cannon Balls 3D is an exciting physics-based demolition game that puts you in control of a powerful cannon! As the master of destruction, your mission is to strategically demolish beautiful buildings with precise shots. The game features realistic 3D physics, allowing you to create spectacular chain reactions as structures collapse. With limited ammunition at your disposal, you'll need to carefully plan each shot to maximize destruction. Use powerful bombs for extra demolition power and watch as buildings crumble in satisfying ways. This engaging game combines strategy, timing, and destruction in a visually stunning package suitable for all ages!

How to Play

  • Aim your cannon carefully at buildings
  • Time your shots for maximum impact
  • Create chain reactions for efficient demolition
  • Manage your limited ammunition wisely
  • Use special bombs strategically
  • Target structural weak points
  • Complete levels with available shots

Game Features

  • Realistic 3D physics engine
  • Beautiful building designs
  • Spectacular chain reactions
  • Various types of ammunition
  • Progressive difficulty levels
  • Strategic demolition gameplay
  • Suitable for all ages

Tips & Strategies

  • Study building structures before shooting
  • Target load-bearing elements
  • Plan for chain reactions
  • Save special bombs for tough levels
  • Watch for structural weak points
  • Use momentum to your advantage
  • Practice precise aiming
